# Inbox triage

## Priorities

1. Messages that need a reply today.
2. Time-sensitive requests, account alerts, and commitments.
3. Messages blocking active work.
4. Useful information that can wait.

## Rules

- Group newsletters, receipts, and automated notifications under FYI.
- Identify the sender, subject, reason it matters, and recommended next action.
- Do not send, delete, archive, label, or modify email.
- Do not include message bodies or private details unless needed to explain the
  required action.
